Frequently asked questions

Can FAQ pages help with voice search optimization?+

FAQ pages use natural language and answer direct questions, making them ideal for voice assistants to pull concise responses to user queries.

What questions should I include on my FAQ page? +

Include genuinely frequently asked questions from customer support, social media, sales teams, and product reviews. Focus on common concerns like order status, shipping, returns, and product details.

Can I integrate a chatbot into my FAQ page? +

IChatbots can provide instant, automated answers to common queries from your FAQ content, reducing support load and offering 24/7 assistance.

How often should I update my FAQ page? +

Update it whenever business changes occur, new products launch, or information becomes outdated. Auditing every three to six months is good practice to ensure accuracy and relevance.
